About

This Website is a trubute to all the cars and trucks I have owned both past and present. I have loved each and everyone of these cars as if they were my own children but my love for cars in general has lead me want to experience as many cars as possible. Cars on this list range from "starter" compact coupes all the way up to muscle cars. Each car on this page contains the (approximate) years I owned them as well as a few statics. For my family and friends who make fun of me, thanks for the idea for a website!
